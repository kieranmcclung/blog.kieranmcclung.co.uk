A little while back, I used to spend my lunchtimes creating inventively titled "Lunchtime Creations". These quick 20-minute designs were good fun, but I fobbed them off in favour of stretching my legs and touching some grass. However, it was raining the other day, and inspiration struck.

I thought it'd be interesting to redesign the Pac-Man logo for a modern age quickly. Blocky sans-serif font. Tick. Remove the character from the original. Tick. Nailed it.
